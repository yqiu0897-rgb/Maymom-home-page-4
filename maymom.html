When the breastfeeding journey begins, life can suddenly feel overwhelming. Excitement and love often arrive hand in hand with uncertainty and questions. Well, you are not alone.

Maymom is here to help

We care about mothers' needs on the path of breastfeeding. We provide breast pump parts and accessories of premium quality, yet, at an affordable price. For the past decade, we listen and fulfill mothers' requests for customized, comfortable and easy-to-use tools. Meanwhile, we are also caring to mothers or babies with special needs.

Maymom products feature safety, efficiency and credibility that you can count on. We are here to help parents to take care of new family members. If you are happy with Maymom products, please tell your friends and families. If you have unease about our products, please let us know. We are always here to assist you.
